Convenience and Connectivity ​

Rohaan Phoenix, situated in Medavakkam, offers residents easy access to a host of amenities and facilities. The proximity of renowned schools like Sujay Public School, Narayana E Techno School, AKG Public School, Saint Joseph Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Vista Billabong High International School, and educational institutions such as Vels Institute of Science, Technology & Advanced Studies and Madras Christian College ensures that families have excellent educational options nearby. For healthcare needs, Sree Balaji Medical College And Deepam Medfirst Hospital is conveniently located. The area also boasts recreational spots like Medavakkam Park and Soumya Nagar Children’s Park, ideal for relaxing and spending quality time with family. When it comes to dining and shopping, residents are spoiled for choice with popular eateries like Namma Veedu Vasanta Bhavan, Geetham Veg Restaurant, Anjappar Chettinad Restaurant, and Dindigul Thalappakatti Restaurant in close proximity. Additionally, Phoenix Mall is just a short drive away, offering a range of shopping and entertainment options. Rohaan Phoenix’s strategic location ensures that residents can enjoy a convenient lifestyle with everything they need just a stone’s throw away.
